Ankita Raina won the Bronze medal in the Asian Games in Indonesia after she lost the Women's Singles Semi Final against Shuai Zhang from China. Zhang beat Raina with a scoreline of 6-4, 7-4. Ankita Raina thus became only the second Indian woman to win a medal at the Asian Games after Sania Mirza, who won a Silver in 2006 and a Bronze in 2010.
